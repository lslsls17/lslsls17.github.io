A Perfect Quant Interview Question

A flexible spending account (FSA) is a type of savings account that provides the account holder with tax advantages to cover health related expenses. Let’s assume you can deposit money at the beginning of the year into such account that only eligible to health-related expenses. The benefit is that all health-related expenses will be tax-exempt. However, the money left-over in the account at year end will be taken away instead of rolling into next year.

The Question is: How much money do you want to deposit into FSA account at the beginning of the year?

Hint: This looks like a very open-ended question. I would suggest you treat it as an optimization question with assumptions. What is the objective function to optimize? What assumptions should we make to start? If you are in the correct track, the question will become an option pricing question with integral derivations.
